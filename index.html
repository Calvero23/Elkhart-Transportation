Quick Questions

Before you book.

Do you offer immediate pickup?

Call for immediate pickup. Phone is the fastest way to confirm driver availability.

Are online ride requests confirmed automatically?

No. A ride is confirmed only after a dispatcher or business representative follows up with you.

Can I schedule an airport or early morning pickup?

Yes. Airport and early pickups should be requested in advance so timing, route, and luggage needs can be confirmed.

Can I schedule a ride for a family member?

Yes. Family members and caregivers can request rides and include appointment, pickup, return ride, and contact details.

What areas do you serve?

Common service areas include Elkhart, Goshen, Mishawaka, South Bend, Granger, Nappanee, Bristol, Middlebury, Osceola, and nearby areas when scheduling allows.

Do you offer medical or hospital transportation?

Medical appointment and hospital rides may be available for private-pay riders, seniors, families, and facility coordination partners.

How do I get a price estimate?

Call with your pickup address, destination, date, time, passenger count, and any special needs. We will confirm current rates and availability.

Can I request a larger vehicle?

Include passenger count and luggage or mobility notes when calling or submitting the form. Vehicle availability should be confirmed before booking.

What should I do if I left something in the vehicle?

Call as soon as possible with your pickup time, route, and contact information so the ride can be looked up.
