How We Work

Built around scheduled, important, and repeat rides.

The goal is not to be just another taxi number. The service model is designed for rides where pickup timing, communication, and accountability matter.

1

Customer calls or sends a ride request with pickup, dropoff, date, time, and ride type.

2

The ride is reviewed for timing, pricing, route needs, and driver availability.

3

The ride is confirmed, assigned, completed, and recorded for payment or billing.

Service area

Primary service is focused on Elkhart, Elkhart County, and nearby Northern Indiana communities. Longer regional rides may be available by quote when timing and driver capacity allow.
